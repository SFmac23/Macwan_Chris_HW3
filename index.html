Peter Griffin

Peter Griffin is the comical, bumbling father in Family Guy, known for his outrageous antics and low intelligence. He works at Pawtucket Brewery and often involves his family and friends in his chaotic adventures. Despite his flaws, he cares deeply for his wife, Lois, and their children, Meg, Chris, and Stewie. His best friends include Quagmire, Cleveland, and Joe, with whom he frequently hangs out at the Drunken Clam.

Stewie Griffin

Stewie Griffin is the youngest member of the Griffin family in Family Guy. He's a baby with a genius-level intellect and a British accent. Known for his elaborate schemes and dark humor, Stewie often plots world domination. Despite his sinister side, he's also endearing and shares a special bond with the family dog, Brian. His unique character brings a witty and mischievous element to the show.
